Your Privacy

User Privacy Choices

Effective date: March 27, 2026

At Articulate, you have control over your personal data. This page outlines the choices available to you regarding the collection, use, and management of your information within the Articulate app.

Your Choices

Audio Recording Data

You choose when to record. Articulate only processes audio you explicitly submit for analysis. We never record in the background or access your microphone without your active participation.

You can delete individual recordings and their associated analysis data at any time from within the app.

Push Notifications

You can opt in or out of push notifications at any time, including reminders, progress updates, and new feature announcements.

Manage in: Settings → Notifications (within the app), or via your device's system notification settings.

Analytics & Usage Data

We collect anonymized usage analytics to improve the app experience. You may opt out of non-essential analytics data collection.

Manage in: Settings → Privacy → Analytics (within the app).

Marketing Communications

If you've signed up for our waitlist or newsletter, you can unsubscribe at any time.

Click the unsubscribe link in any marketing email, or contact us directly.

Account & Data Deletion

You may request complete deletion of your account and all associated data at any time. Once processed, this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Request via: Settings → Account → Delete Account (within the app), or by emailing us at the address below.

Third-Party Services

Articulate may integrate with third-party services for analytics, hosting, and infrastructure. These services have their own privacy policies. We only work with providers that maintain appropriate data protection standards.

Do Not Track

Articulate respects Do Not Track ("DNT") signals sent by your browser. When a DNT signal is detected, we disable non-essential tracking and analytics.

California Residents

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information is collected, to request its deletion, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. Articulate does not sell personal information.

EU/EEA Residents

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including access, rectification, erasure, data portability, and the right to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us below.

Updates

We may update this page as our app and features evolve. Material changes will be communicated through the app or via email.

Have questions or want to exercise your privacy rights?

Contact privacy@usearticulate.app
